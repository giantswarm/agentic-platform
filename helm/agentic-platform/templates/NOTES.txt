CRD lifecycle note:
  `helm uninstall {{ .Release.Name }}` will delete the agentgateway CRDs because
  upstream does not annotate them with `helm.sh/resource-policy: keep`. Any
  AgentgatewayParameters / AgentgatewayBackend / AgentgatewayPolicy CRs will be
  cascade-deleted. Set `agentgateway-crds.enabled=false` and manage the CRDs
  out-of-band if you need uninstall safety.
